About Me!

To be honest, UX design wasn’t even my first choice for a career. I wanted to be a chemical engineer before, which is something vastly different, but as I was taking my courses I suddenly realized that I don’t really like chemistry. Having realized that I wouldn’t be happy going down this path, I had to find another path to transition towards. Long story short, I eventually decided on UX design, reason being, I am a person who heavily values positive and clean user experience. So being able to create and design a well-crafted and positive user experience fills me with a sense of fulfilment, which is something I didn’t realize at the time when I was looking for a new door to open. I even have a personal wordmark that I created using my first name (it’s the one in the top left), and I am very happy with how it turned out because I think it perfectly encompasses the values I feel are important to me.
