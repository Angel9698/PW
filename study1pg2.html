Case Study #2

box

COSRX is a skincare brand known for its values of minimalism, transparency, and trust. However, an evaluation of their website revealed usability issues that conflicted with these core principles.
This project focused on identifying those issues and redesigning key pages to create a more intuitive, trustworthy user experience.

    Problem

    High cognitive load

  • Too much information presented at once
  • Lack of chunking or progressive disclosure

    • Betrayed trust

  • Hidden scam warnings
  • Absence of ingredients list

    Design solutions

    Streamlined design

  • Focused on a clean, minimal interface
  • Removed unnecessary visual clutter

    • Clear visual hierarchy

  • Essential information was re-arranged
  • Made headings and sub-headings more prominent

    Impact

  • Strengthened alignment with COSRX’s brand values
  • Made product information easier to understand
  • Reduced cognitive load through structured content
  • increased user engagement & loyalty
