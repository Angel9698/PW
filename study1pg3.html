Case Study #1

box

Originally, my portfolio lacked any storytelling, clear hierarchy, and a compelling first impression.
This project focused on redesigning my portfolio to better communicate my value and showcase my work more effectively.

    Problem

    Weak first impression

  • Generic landing page didn't communicate who I am
  • Missing a call-to-action

    • Flat presentation

  • Case studies focused on visuals
  • Lack of depth

    Design solutions

    Landing page redesign

  • Clear headline describes my role and focus
  • Include a call-to-action

    • Case study overhaul

  • Case studies now follow a clear structure
  • Shows my thinking process, not just what I designed
  • Included before and after images

    Impact

  • Improved understanding of my skills
  • Better engagement with my projects
  • Clear structure and storytelling
